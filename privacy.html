Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

🔒 AutoNest is committed to protecting your privacy. We never sell your personal data to third parties. This policy explains exactly what we collect and why.

1Introduction

AutoNest Technologies Pvt. Ltd. ("AutoNest", "we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Platform.

This Policy is in compliance with India's Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011, and the upcoming Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

2Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

3How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To process your bookings, confirm appointments, and connect you with service providers.
  • Account Management: To create and maintain your account, authenticate your identity, and enable profile features.
  • Payments & Billing: To process transactions securely and issue refunds when applicable.
  • Communications: To send booking confirmations, reminders, receipts, and important service updates via email and SMS.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your queries, resolve disputes, and improve our support quality.
  • Platform Improvement: To analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, and develop new features.
  • Marketing (with consent): To send promotional offers and newsletters. You can opt out at any time.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, court orders, or regulatory requirements.

We will not use your data for any purpose beyond what is listed here without obtaining your explicit consent first.

4Data Protection

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • All data transmission is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS 1.2+.
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we never store plaintext passwords.
  • Database access is restricted to authorized personnel only, with role-based access controls.
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments are conducted.
  • Our servers are hosted on trusted cloud infrastructure with physical security and redundancy.

Despite these measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach that affects your rights, we will notify you as required by applicable law within 72 hours of discovery.

5Cookies & Tracking

AutoNest uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on the Platform. Here's what we use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the Platform to function (e.g., session management, authentication). Cannot be disabled.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings such as theme (light/dark mode) and language.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the Platform (e.g., Google Analytics). Data is anonymised.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to show relevant advertisements on third-party platforms. Only active if you consent.

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect Platform functionality. We use localStorage for certain user preferences which does not involve server-side tracking.

6Third-party Services

We work with trusted third-party services to operate the Platform. When you use AutoNest, your data may be shared with:

  • Razorpay: For secure payment processing. Razorpay handles all card and UPI transaction data. See Razorpay's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Maps API: For location services and station mapping. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Analytics: For anonymised usage analytics to improve the Platform.
  • SMS / Email Providers: For sending transactional notifications. Data shared is limited to recipient contact details.
  • Partner Service Providers: Your name, vehicle number, and booking details are shared with the specific partner you book a service with. They are bound by confidentiality agreements.

We do not sell your personal data to any third party. Sharing with partners is limited to what is necessary to fulfill your booking.

7Your Rights

Under applicable data protection law, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct any inaccurate or incomplete data in your profile.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your account and associated data (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Opt out of marketing communications at any time via the unsubscribe link in emails.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data for marketing or profiling purposes.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@autonest.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Specifically:

  • Account Data: Retained for the duration of your account plus 2 years after deletion (for legal compliance).
  • Transaction Records: Retained for 7 years as required under Indian financial regulations.
  • Support Conversations: Retained for 2 years.
  • Analytics Data: Anonymised and aggregated after 26 months.

After the applicable retention period, data is securely deleted or anonymised. You may request earlier deletion as per your rights in Section 7.

9Children's Privacy

AutoNest is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at privacy@autonest.com and we will delete the information promptly.
