Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about AutoNest

🔍
🚗 General

AutoNest is a smart auto services platform based in Nashik, Maharashtra. We connect car owners with EV charging stations, trusted mechanics, CNG booking slots, and premium car wash services — all in one app. Our goal is to make vehicle maintenance effortless and transparent.

AutoNest currently operates in Nashik, Maharashtra. We're actively expanding to Pune, Mumbai, and other Tier-2 cities in 2025. Stay tuned to our announcements for updates.

You can browse available services and stations without an account. However, to make bookings, track orders, and make payments, you'll need to create a free account. Registration takes under a minute.

Our web application is fully mobile-responsive and works great on all smartphones. A dedicated Android and iOS app is currently in development and will be launched soon. You can add our website to your home screen as a PWA in the meantime.

💳 Payments

We accept all major payment methods including UPI (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm), credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), and net banking. All payments are securely processed via Razorpay. Cash payments are not accepted on the platform.

Refunds are automatically initiated for eligible cancellations. If you cancel a booking more than 2 hours before your scheduled slot, you'll receive a full refund within 5–7 business days to your original payment method. For cancellations under 2 hours, a 20% cancellation fee applies. Contact support for disputed charges.

Payment failures can happen due to: insufficient balance, incorrect card details, network timeout, or bank-side issues. If money was debited but booking wasn't confirmed, the amount will be auto-refunded within 3–5 business days. If not, contact us at support@autonest.com.

No hidden charges. The price shown on the service page is the final amount. Some premium services may include a small convenience fee which is always displayed transparently before checkout.

On the checkout page, you'll see a "Apply Promo Code" field. Enter your code and click Apply. Valid codes will instantly reduce your total. Promo codes are case-insensitive and can usually be used only once per account.

📦 Orders & Services

You can track your booking in real-time by going to Orders in the navigation menu. Each booking shows its current status: Confirmed, In Progress, Completed, or Cancelled. You'll also receive WhatsApp/SMS notifications at each stage.

Yes. Go to your Orders page, select the booking, and click "Cancel" or "Reschedule." Rescheduling is free if done more than 2 hours before the slot. Cancellations close to the slot time may incur a small fee (see our Cancellation Policy).

You can book up to 7 days in advance for CNG slots and car wash services. EV charging sessions can be reserved up to 24 hours ahead. Mechanic appointments can be booked up to 14 days in advance.

Auto-cancellations happen when a service slot becomes unavailable (e.g., station maintenance) or when your payment couldn't be confirmed. In such cases, a full refund is processed automatically. You can rebook the service at your convenience.

🔐 Account & Security

On the Login page, click "Forgot Password." Enter your registered email and we'll send a reset link. The link is valid for 30 minutes. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

Log in and navigate to My Profile. From there you can edit your name, phone number, vehicle details, and address. Email changes require verification via a link sent to your new email address.

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for all data transmission and store passwords using bcrypt hashing. Payment data is never stored on our servers — it's handled entirely by Razorpay. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

You can request account deletion by emailing support@autonest.com with your registered email. We'll process the request within 7 business days. Note: active bookings must be completed or cancelled before deletion.

🛠️ Technical Issues

Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+R). If the issue persists, try a different browser or disable browser extensions. Our platform supports the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. If you're still facing issues, contact our tech support.

Check that you've allowed browser notifications for AutoNest (click the lock icon in your address bar → Notifications → Allow). Also ensure your phone is not in Do Not Disturb mode. SMS notifications should arrive regardless of browser settings.

Make sure you've granted location permission to AutoNest in your browser settings. Also ensure your GPS/location is turned on. If you're in an area with poor GPS signal, try switching to "Manual Search" mode where you can type your area name directly.

Our listings are updated regularly by partner providers. If you spot incorrect information (wrong timings, price, or availability), please use the "Report Issue" button on the service page or email us. We typically update listings within 24 hours.
